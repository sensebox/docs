---
title: Initial Setup of the senseBox:bike 
---

Follow these steps for a smooth initial setup:

## Unpack and Prepare Your senseBox:bike

- You can find detailed step-by-step instructions on how to do that [here](https://docs.sensebox.de/en/docs/products/bike/halterung/installation).

## Charge and Power On the Device

- Before your first ride, fully charge the senseBox:bike using the included USB cable. A full charge ensures optimal battery life (typically 4–8 hours of operation).
- Press the power button to turn on the senseBox:bike.
- The device will indicate it is ready when you see the QR code on the LED display.

## Install the senseBox:bike App

- Download the senseBox:bike app from the [Google Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=de.hochschuletrier.bikeapp) or [Apple App Store](https://apps.apple.com/de/app/bikeapp/id1530130134). 
- Open the app, ensure Bluetooth is enabled on your smartphone and grant app all the reqeusted permissions.

## 5*. Link your openSenseMap account

- Register a new openSenseMap account or log in to an existing one.
- Create a new box and make sure it's selected. Please make sure that you use the correct box type and campaign if you are part of one.

:::info
Note: At the moment box and openSenseMap account management is only available over [website](https://opensensemap.org/).
:::

## Connect the App to Your Device

- In the app, click the "Connect" button and select your senseBox from the list.
- Once paired, you’ll see live sensor data displayed in the app.

## Start Gathering Data

- Click the "Start" button and begin your ride!

:::info
Note: For privacy, you can set up Privacy Zone(s) in the app where no data will be collected or uploaded (e.g., around your home or office). You can set up your Privacy Zone(s) on the Settings screen.
:::

If you encounter any issues during setup, refer to the official [senseBox:bike documentation for troubleshooting](https://docs.sensebox.de/en/docs/products/bike/app/troubleshooting/) or [contact us](email:kontakt@sensebox.de).
